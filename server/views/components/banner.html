India Dapp Fest 2019

Asia's top-notch revelation on blockchain based applications

IDF is a 5 day mega scale festival happening from 11-15th June, 2019 in Bangalore, India. The first 3 days will comprise of a multi-track hackathon and interesting workshops(ex crypto trading 101, developer adoption) and last 2 days will comprise of high quality conference. It will be the largest dapp focussed conference in Asia, having an audience of 1500+ people, out of which 750+ should be developers.
